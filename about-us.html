About Us

TechMania specializes in selling electronic products such as computers and smartphones at affordable prices with international shipping. We also offer other services such as repairing devices and modifications of certain brands of smartphones or consoles.
Our company was first set up in early 2017 in Mauritius. Currently, we have two franchises around the globe; one in America and another one in China.
At TechMania, we firmly believe that our mission is to interconnect the world by providing high-end devices to people regardless of their financial background. Hence, throughout years of trial and error, we have been able to devise and refine our business model in such a way to allow us to do so.
